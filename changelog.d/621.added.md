First-class **metabolism** — a chamber's self-reported, intentional context-throughput state, the layer-3 axis that complements the auto-observed `agent_state` probe and the operator-flag `attention_state`. Three states the pane-observer cannot infer: `warming` (just resumed, not yet at full throughput), `saturating` (context-load approaching the `/compact`-need), `compact-pending` (intent-to-`/compact` stated but not yet fired). A chamber self-reports via `tmux-tell.set_metabolism` (MCP) or `tmux-tell-claude set-metabolism <value>` / `--clear` (CLI) — **self-only**: the call resolves the caller's own identity and exposes no target, so a chamber can only set its own metabolism (a third-party write would clobber the target's real signal). The self-report is advisory (it never gates delivery) and surfaces alongside the observed state in `agent_state`, plus a `METABOLISM` column in `agents`. `compact-pending` auto-clears once the mailman observes the chamber actually `at-rest-in-compaction` (observed-truth supersedes the redundant self-report).
